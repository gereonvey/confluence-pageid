Confluence PageId Bookmarklet

Sometimes confluence uses links like https://.../viewpage.action?pageId=123456789 and sometimes it uses something like https://.../My+Page. I think the former style of links is much more desirable, as the latter breaks on any page move or renaming.

Here is how you get to the pageId link easily:

  1. Bookmark the following link, so that it appears in your bookmarks bar: Confluence PageId
  2. Navigate to a Confluence page
  3. Click on the bookmarklet and the URL will change to the version using pageId
